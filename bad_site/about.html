About me, I am a junior developer from Edmonton, with a particular interest in video games, and video game development. I will be graduating from the University of Alberta this year with a Specialization in Computing Science. Programming in itself, and the challenges that it brings I find can be fun and exciting to tackle once you're in the thick of it.

When it comes to development, I prefer working as part of a structured team with outlined roles as I find that it greatly improves speed and quality of work over several individuals working alone and then piecing what they have together.

Outside of school, my hobbies include playing video games competitively and casually. I also enjoy the Trading Card Game, Magic the Gathering, though admittedly I'm not very good at it!
